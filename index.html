Open-Minded

This project aims to carry out a website and a native mobile application for Android and IOS, where the demographic information of the students is managed, so that the information can be consulted, deleted, edited and searched. With the objective of organizing information for the administrative area of a university using information structures.

Technologies used:

Characteristics:

Embark on the starting point, a place full of possibilities where you can perform various actions ranging from adding a student, viewing the list of admitted students, receiving alerts about the protection of personal data to discovering fascinating details about the project. Once students are added, the opportunity to edit and explore each student's information opens up, providing an enriching journey through their profiles.

Immerse yourself in this dedicated space, where you have the power to shape information according to your needs, whether editing details or deleting records. Before taking the deletion action, the app will guide you with a careful alert, making sure you are fully aware of every step you take in the journey of managing student information.
